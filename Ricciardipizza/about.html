    About Us

    Welcome to Ricciardi's Pizzaria and Bar, where the heart of Chicago's iconic culinary tradition comes alive in every slice! Nestled in the center of Richmond Hill Georgia, we are proud to serve deep dish pizza that is renowned as one of the top three in the state. Whether you're a local or a visitor, our restaurant is a must-visit destination for an authentic taste of Chicago.

    Join us at CRicciardi's Pizzaria and Bar and experience the true taste of Chicago. We can't wait to serve you and make you a part of our family.

    Our Story

    Ricciardi's Pizzaria and Bar was born out of a passion for creating the perfect deep dish pizza. A symphony of flavors that pays homage to Chicago's rich food heritage. Our founder, Chris Ricciardi, a Chicago native, combined family recipes with innovative techniques to craft a menu that celebrates both tradition and creativity. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to bringing people together over great food, fostering a warm and welcoming environment for all.

    Our Food

    Our deep dish pizza is more than just a meal; it's an experience. Each pizza is handcrafted with love, using only the finest ingredients. From our tangy, house-made tomato sauce to our rich, gooey cheese and perfectly baked crust, every bite is a testament to our commitment to quality. Beyond pizza, our menu features a variety of Italian-American classics, ensuring there's something to satisfy every palate.

    Our Atmosphere

    At Ricciardi's Pizzaria and Bar, we believe that great food should be enjoyed in a great setting. Our restaurant is designed to be both adult and kid-friendly, offering a cozy, inviting space that appeals to all ages. Whether you're here for a family dinner, or a casual night out with friends, you'll find our atmosphere to be the perfect blend of lively and laid-back. Kids will love our special menu and fun activities, while adults can unwind with a selection of local craft beers and mixed drinks.

    Our Commitment

    We are committed to more than just serving delicious food. At Ricciardi's Pizzaria and Bar, we strive to create a community where everyone feels at home. We take pride in our friendly, attentive service and our involvement in local events. Our goal is to not only be the best deep dish pizza restaurant in the state but also a beloved part of the Richmond Hill community.

    Join us at Ricciardi's Pizzaria and Bar and experience the true taste of Chicago. We can't wait to serve you and make you a part of our family.
